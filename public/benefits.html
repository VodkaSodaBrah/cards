Worth knowing (verified)
  • United Explorer's $150 annual fee is waived your first year only. After that, the $100 hotel credit + $60 rideshare + $50 rental + free bag savings need to add up to clear it.
  • NavFed Amex acceptance in Mexico is meaningfully worse than Visa. Don't rely on it as your only card south of the border.
  • CFU has a 3% foreign transaction fee. Don't use abroad.
  • CSP's 3x online grocery excludes Walmart, Target, and wholesale clubs (those earn 1x).
  • NavFed 3x category requires correct merchant coding — purchases at superstores, supermarkets, or warehouse clubs for gas may only earn 1x. Same for restaurants inside hotels/casinos/event venues.
  • United Explorer purchase protection of $10K/item is unusually high — use it for any major electronics or appliance purchases.
  • Chase 5/24 rule: 5+ personal cards (any issuer) opened in 24 months = Chase denies new apps.
  • United Explorer has a 1,000 PQP/yr cap — small contribution toward Premier status but it exists.
